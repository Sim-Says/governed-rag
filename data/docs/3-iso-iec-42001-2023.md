## 3. ISO/IEC 42001:2023

**What it is:** First international standard for AI management systems (AIMS), published Dec 2023. The AI equivalent of ISO 27001 for infosec. Certifiable management system standard for organizations developing/using AI.

### Structure
- **Clauses 4–10** (PDCA management system, same structure as ISO 27001/9001):
  - Clause 4: Context of the organization
  - Clause 5: Leadership & AI policy
  - Clause 6: Planning (AI risk assessment, AI risk treatment, objectives)
  - Clause 7: Support (resources, competence, awareness, data management)
  - Clause 8: Operation (AI lifecycle controls, impact assessment, deployment, monitoring)
  - Clause 9: Performance evaluation (monitoring, internal audit, management review)
  - Clause 10: Improvement (nonconformity, corrective action)
- **Annex A:** 38 controls across 9 domains (AI policies, internal org, resource management, data management, impact assessments, AI lifecycle, transparency, third-party relationships, communication)
- **Annex B:** Implementation guidance mapped to AI lifecycle stages

### Key Concepts
AI Impact Assessment (AIIA — analogous to DPIA), AI risk treatment plan, AI lifecycle approach (concept → design → data → dev → testing → deployment → operation → decommission), data management controls, third-party/supplier AI risk.
