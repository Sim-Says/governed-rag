## 6. MeitY AI Governance (India)

**What it is:** India has not enacted a standalone AI Act. Instead, MeitY has integrated AI governance into existing digital frameworks through IT Rules amendments, AI Governance Guidelines, and the IndiaAI Mission. The Digital India Act remains in draft.

### Key Instruments (Updated 2026)
- **IT Rules Amendment (2026):** Enacted Feb 20, 2026. Mandates that synthetically generated information (SGI) and deepfakes must be prominently labeled. Platforms required to take down flagged illegal content and deepfakes within **2–3 hour windows**.
- **India AI Governance Guidelines:** Released by MeitY to establish stages for future AI regulations. Provides recommendations for industry and regulators, emphasizing ethics by design.
- **IndiaAI Mission (approved March 2024):** ₹10,372 crore — includes AI safety, governance, responsible AI. Establishes AI Safety Institute concept.
- **Digital India Act (proposed, not enacted):** Draft since 2023, aims to replace IT Act 2000. Would regulate AI, deepfakes, algorithmic harm broadly. Still under legislative review — no timeline for passage.
- **AI Ethics and Accountability Bill:** Private member's bill introduced late 2025 — not passed into law.
- **DPDP Rules, 2025:** Notified Nov 2025 — see Section 4 above.
- **Responsible AI for All (NITI Aayog, 2021 → evolved):** Principles — safety, reliability, inclusivity, non-discrimination, transparency, accountability, privacy.
